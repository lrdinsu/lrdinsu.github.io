Hello! I'm **Lynn Wang**, a software engineer passionate about exploring the mechanics of system design and the process of building reliable applications.

This blog serves as a space where I document my learning journey, share technical deep dives, and discuss the architectural challenges I encounter while building software.

## Focus Areas

My technical interests and the primary topics of this blog include:

- **System Design:** Exploring architectural patterns and the trade-offs required to build scalable systems.
- **Software Engineering:** Best practices, clean code, and effective development workflows.
- **Reliable Applications:** Techniques for creating robust, resilient, and maintainable software.

I started this blog to bridge the gap between abstract concepts and practical implementation.
Whether you're a fellow engineer or just curious about how complex systems work, I hope you find these insights valuable.
